NEW- MINT MONO THREADS FROM $500

Mint mono threads are made of Polydioxanone. These create a vectorial network within the skin that supports skin tissue. They provide visible skin tightening whilst stimulating natural collagen production with the skin. The skin is rejuvenated, improvement in vitality, elasticity and skin tone are all noted. Treatment takes 30-45 minutes, a topical anaesthetic is used. Threads take 3-4 months to dissolve and this is when ultimate results are seen. Longevity of results is 9-12 months. SKIN TREATMENTS

Embrace Aesthetics believes in using cosmeceutical skin care in combination with effective yet gentle skin treatments to get your skin where you want it to be. Skin care doesn’t have to be complex or over priced. Let Freya get you started on your skin journey – book a free consultation.

Below is information about each treatment. If you are still unsure as to what to book, just book a skin consultation to begin with and I can guide you appropriately.

LYMPHATIC THERAPY

Antioxidant based treatment mask that hardens and tightens on the skin. This mask draws blood flow to the surface of the skin and assists with lymphatic drainage. By creating a clear pathway we can increase the delivery of oxygen, water and nutrients delivered to skin cells and detoxify the skin.

Perfect for pre event prep due to glowing results. Excellent for all skin types to brighten, refresh, reduce redness, acne and rehydrate the skin. Great combination treatment.

There is no prep required for this treatment. It takes 1 hour and involves no down time. 1 treatment for a pick me up is perfect otherwise a course is advised for more serious skin concerns.

$160 per treatment

Book Now

SKIN NEEDLING

Dermapen/skin needling is a corrective treatment that creates rejuvenating micro-channels into the skin’s matrix. This treatment is also known at times as “Collagen Induction Therapy” as we are causing controlled trauma to induce collagen production.

The treatment aims to improve the appearance of ageing, wrinkles, enlarged pores, loose skin, uneven skin tone and texture and scar tissue.

The treatment takes approximately 45 minutes.

It is advised to do 3-6 sessions to see changes spaced from 4 to 6 weeks apart. Redness can occur to the skin for up 72 hours post treatment. Tightness, sensitivity and dryness can occur up to one week post treatment.

$290 per treatment

LED THERAPY

Introducing the Medilux LED device.

Clinically proven LED (Light Emitting Diodes) therapy is effective for skin rejuvenation, acne reduction, hyperpigmentation and skin detoxification. Our dermis will convert light to ‘adenosine triphosphate’ which in turn will help with the production of both collagen and elastin.Various therapeutic wavelengths of light are produced and initiate a cellular response.

Not only are the long lasting benefits of LED amazing but the immediate glow you experience afterward is undeniably addictive

This treatment is great for all skin types and involves no down time. Great for a one off pick me up or a course of 6 is advised.

$90 per treatment

CHEMICAL PEELS

A course of chemical peels are great to improve the appearance of skin imperfections such as, fine lines and wrinkles, irregular texture, dull and sallow skin, hyperpigmentation and acne.

A peel provides an exfoliation of the outer most layer of the skin, which in turn stimulates both cellular turnover and the internal structure of the skin, to activate and energise fibroblasts and collagen synthesis.

I advise you to organise your Chemical Peel treatment 10 days prior to any important events. A course of 6-10 treatments is generally advised. At times certain peels do require you to prepare your skin with product so a skin consultation prior to your treatment is always essential.

From $90 per treatment

Book Now

DELUXE MICRODERMABRASION &SONOPHORESIS

Microdermabrasion is a non-surgical, safe and gentle procedure that exfoliates and polishes the skin on the face and body to stimulate new cell growth.

Combining a gentle vacuum system and diamond tips, Microdermabrasion removes dead skin cells and unclogs pores, to reveal a younger, fresher complexion. It is a non-invasive, pain free procedure which can be used to treat a number of skincare concerns and requires no down time.

Sonophoresis is a safe, gentle treatment that uses ultrasonic technology, warmth and motion energy to significantly increase the depth and absorption rate of active ingredients into the deeper layers of the skin.

The beauty of microdermabrasion is that you see a change in your skin immediately! It can be used as a stand alone treatment or in a course of other Microdermabrasions or skin treatments.

I advise you to organise your Microdermabrasion treatment 10 days prior to any important events. Great as a one off maintenance treatment!

$120 per treatment
